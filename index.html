Hyundai-H1 Information

Our smallest shuttle is remarkably comfortable and modern. Despite its compact size, it doesn't sacrifice on comfort, making it an ideal choice for small groups or families. With plush seating, ample legroom, and modern amenities, the Hyundai-H1 ensures a pleasant and enjoyable travel experience for up to 6 passengers.

Vehicle Features

  • Capacity: 1-6 passengers
  • Comfortable seats with plush cushions
  • Ample legroom for a relaxing ride
  • Modern entertainment system for passenger enjoyment
  • Air-conditioned interior for a comfortable climate
  • Wi-Fi connectivity to stay connected on the go
  • Ample luggage storage for belongings of up to 6 suitcases

Toyota Hiace
7-10 passengers

Toyota Hiace Information

The Toyota Hiace is a spacious and versatile shuttle designed for 7 to 10 passengers. The Hiace offers comfortable seats, ample legroom and modern amenities to ensure a pleasant and convenient journey for all passengers. Whether it's a short trip within the city or a long-distance adventure, the Toyota Hiace ensures a smooth and pleasant travel experience for everyone on board.

Vehicle Features

  • Capacity: 7-10 passengers
  • Premium leather seats for a luxurious experience
  • Extra legroom for added comfort during long trips
  • Advanced audio system with surround sound
  • Climate control for individual temperature preferences
  • Enhanced safety features for a secure journey
  • Stylish interior design with modern aesthetics
  • Large panoramic windows for scenic views
